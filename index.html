Happy International Women's Day
Women in Machine Learning & Data Science (WiMLDS) Goa is glad to invite you for a week-long celebration of Women in Technology. Join us from 8th -14th March virtually to hear from some of the finest women speakers and industry pioneers of our country. Be sure to expect a mix of Tech, Non-tech and fun sessions.




Speakers

 Usha Rengaraju - Kaggle 101

 Hear from the grandmaster herself what kaggle is all about and how you can use it to enhance your  data science skills. Join us on 8th March@6pm IST.


 

Vritika Naik-Networking, the secret to hacking Opportunities

Find out how networking can be beneficial to grow your network , how you should go about it, the tools and stand out from the crowd. Join us on 9th March@7pm IST


 

 Dikshita Desai- TinyML

 To all the Machine Learning Enthusiasts out there, join us for a very interesting session on "TinyML" and explore what it is all about. Join us on 10th March @ 9:30pm IST.


 

Nikita Mallya - PM Talks with Nikita

If you've always been confused whether product management is something you want to look at as a career but end up with tons of questions? Join us for a very interactive session on Product Management and hear from Nikita about her journey in Product Management.
Join in on 11th March @ 9:30pm IST.


 

 Steffie Tom- Gear your Career with Leadership

 Explore why leadership becomes an essential skill at workplace and how you can mold your career with it. Join in on 12th March @ 8:30pm IST.


 

 Kavya Priyanka Kakinanda - Recommendation Systems

 Gear up for the last day of WiMLDS Week with a session on Recommendation Systems. Learn about how AI is leverages user needs by the use of powerful Recommendation Engines in both consumer and vendor side across sectors. Join in on 14th March @ 6pm IST

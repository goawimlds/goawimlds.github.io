Registration Steps

Kindly follow all these steps for a successful registration to WiMLDS Week

1

Register on Meetup◹

If you do not have an account on Meetup, kindly register. Skip this step if you already have an account.

2

Join the WiMLDS Goa Meetup group◹

Kindly join the group as Event Details will only be visible to members of the group. All event updates will be sent to members only.

3

Fill the google form◹

As a final step, kindly fill this google form as it will help us contact you.

4

Attend Event

An event meetup will be created for each day and all the members on meetup would be notified. Click on "Attend Online" for each day. Only once you click this, the meet link to the virtual meet will be visible to you.
If you need further assistance then kindly contact us at goa.wimlds@gmail.com◹
